Loch End Corners Conceptual Scheme


On July 14, 2011 our team met with Rocky View County to formally go through the first reading of the conceptual scheme (direction given by Rocky View Council in April 2008). This first reading process facilitates the scheduling of dates for the public hearing. The public hearing date at the County has been set as September 6, 2011, wherein the second and third readings will take place. At that time, council will view our interaction with the community, review all our technical data and vote on the merits of the Lochend properties.
