Welcome to the website ForTheCommunity.ca!

We live in Bearspaw and we work in Bearspaw. You are our community. The purpose of this site is to gain insight from residents of Bearspaw and to provide information on the background, process and concepts to be delivered on the two quarter sections on the Northeast and Southeast of Lochend Road and Hwy 262.

We ask that you take five minutes and fill out the questionnaire so that we can incorporate your thoughts into the data we are collecting for the project. Those that complete the questionnaire and provide their email address will receive an overview of the final results. We have worked with the residents of Bearspaw for 5 years so we have a good understanding of what are the most important factors effecting a broad range of people throughout the area. We want to make sure we haven't missed anything so we welcome your thoughts.
